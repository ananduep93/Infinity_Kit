Introduction

This tool allows you to rotate the pages of your PDF document to the correct orientation, fixing any sideways or upside-down pages with ease.

🛠️

How to Use

  1. Upload the PDF document you want to fix.
  2. Choose your desired rotation angle (90°, 180°, or 270°).
  3. Download your updated and correctly oriented PDF file.
✨

Features

  • Easy rotation: A simple way to fix page orientation in seconds.
  • Quick process: No waiting—rotation happens almost instantly in your browser.
  • Simple interface: Designed for clarity and ease of use for everyone.
💡

Example

If you have a sideways scanned page, simply rotate it by 90° or 270° to fix its orientation perfectly.

🚀

Why Use This Tool?

It's the perfect utility for fixing poorly scanned documents and ensuring your PDFs look professional and are easy to read on any device.
