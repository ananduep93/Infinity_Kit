Introduction

This tool helps you quickly identify the type of a triangle based on the lengths of its three sides, making geometry problems easier to solve.

🛠️

How to Use

  1. Enter the lengths of all three sides of the triangle.
  2. Click the Check button to analyze the sides.
  3. View the identified triangle type and its properties instantly.
✨

Features

  • Fast result: Get an immediate classification of your triangle.
  • Easy input: Simple fields for inputting all three side lengths.
  • Accurate: Uses precise geometric logic to determine the correct type.
💡

Example

If you enter three equal sides (e.g., 5, 5, 5), the tool will identify it as an Equilateral triangle.

🚀

Why Use This Tool?

It's an excellent educational resource for students learning about geometry and the different classifications of triangles.

❓

FAQ

Q: Types of triangle? ▼
The tool can identify Equilateral (all sides equal), Isosceles (two sides equal), and Scalene (no sides equal) triangles.
Q: Is it correct? ▼
Yes, the tool applies standard geometric rules and the triangle inequality theorem to ensure accuracy.
