Introduction

This tool helps you quickly and easily log your daily expenditures, allowing you to track exactly where your money is going and manage your finances better.

🛠️

How to Use

  1. Enter the total amount of your expense.
  2. Select the appropriate category (e.g., Food, Travel).
  3. Optionally, add a note for more details.
  4. Click the Add button to save your entry.
✨

Features

  • Quick entry: Add expenses in a matter of seconds.
  • Category selection: Organize your spending with pre-defined or custom categories.
  • Easy to use: A simple and intuitive interface for daily tracking.
  • Instant save: Your data is stored immediately for future review.
💡

Example

Log a ₹100 expense under the Food category and it will be saved to your list instantly.

🚀

Why Use This Tool?

Consistently tracking your daily spending is the first step toward better money management and financial awareness.
