FAQ

Q: What is LCM? ▼
LCM (Least Common Multiple) is the smallest number that is a multiple of two or more numbers.
Q: What is HCF? ▼
HCF (Highest Common Factor) is the largest positive integer that divides each of the integers without a remainder.
