Introduction

This tool helps you set a financial limit for your spending and track your progress in real-time, helping you stay within your means and reach your savings goals.

🛠️

How to Use

  1. Set your total budget amount for the period.
  2. Continue adding your expenses as they occur.
  3. Monitor your remaining balance to see how much you have left to spend.
✨

Features

  • Budget limit: Set a clear upper boundary for your expenditures.
  • Expense tracking: Automatically see how each new expense affects your overall budget.
  • Simple design: A focused and clean interface for straightforward tracking.
💡

Example

If you set a Budget of ₹5000 and have Spent ₹3000, the tool will show a Remaining Balance of ₹2000.

Why Use This Tool?

It is a powerful ally in the fight against overspending, giving you the immediate feedback you need to stay on track financially.
