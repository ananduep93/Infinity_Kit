Introduction

This tool helps you make random choices in a fun and interactive way using a virtual spinning wheel.

🛠️

How to Use

  1. Enter all the options you want to choose from.
  2. Click the Spin button to start the wheel.
  3. Wait for the wheel to stop and see your random result.
✨

Features

  • Random selection: Purely random outcomes for fair decisions.
  • Fun to use: Engaging animation and sound effects.
  • Easy input: Quickly add or remove options as needed.
  • Fast result: Get your answer in just a few seconds.
💡

Example

Can't decide between Option A, B, or C? Add them to the wheel, spin, and let the tool pick Result B for you!

🚀

Why Use This Tool?

It's perfect for games, giveaways, classroom activities, or whenever you need a quick and unbiased way to pick a winner.

FAQ

Absolutely! You can add as many choices as you need, and the wheel will adjust automatically.
