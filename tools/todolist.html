Introduction

This tool helps you organize your day by managing your tasks in one place. You can easily add, track, and complete your daily goals.

🛠️

How to Use

  1. Type your task into the input field.
  2. Click the Add button to save it to your list.
  3. Mark each task as done by clicking its status when you complete it.
✨

Features

  • Easy task management: Add, edit, or delete tasks with just a few clicks.
  • Mark as complete: Clearly see your progress by crossing off finished items.
  • Simple interface: Designed for clarity so you can focus on your work.
  • No login needed: Start organizing immediately without any registration.
💡

Example

Add a task like "Study for exam" and then mark it as done once you have finished studying.

🚀

Why Use This Tool?

It is the perfect way to stay organized, reduce stress, and ensure that all your important work is completed on time.
