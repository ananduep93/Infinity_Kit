Introduction

This tool calculates your Body Mass Index (BMI) using your height and weight, providing an instant assessment of your body weight relative to your height.

  1. Enter your height in the specified units.
  2. Enter your current weight.
  3. Click the Calculate button to see your result instantly.

  • Easy input: User-friendly interface for quick entry.
  • Health category: Provides immediate feedback on your health category.

FAQ

Q: What is BMI? ▼
BMI is a measure that uses your height and weight to work out if your weight is healthy.
Q: Is it accurate? ▼
Yes, it is highly accurate for general body weight assessment in adults.
