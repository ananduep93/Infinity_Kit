Loading tool...

Introduction

This tool helps you analyze the strength of your passwords, giving you an immediate score and feedback on how to make them more secure against hacking attempts.

🛠️

How to Use

  1. Enter the password you want to check into the input field.
  2. Instantly see the strength result (Weak, Medium, or Strong).
  3. Follow any suggestions provided to improve your password's security.
✨

Features

  • Instant check: Get immediate feedback as you type your password.
  • Simple result: Easy-to-understand visual indicators of password quality.
  • Easy to use: A straightforward process for anyone to check their security.
💡

Example

Entering a simple password like "12345" will result in a Weak score, while a complex one like "A@9kL#2x" will be rated as Strong.

🚀

Why Use This Tool?

It's an essential security utility that helps you create much safer passwords, protecting your personal accounts from common cyber threats.

❓

FAQ

Q: Is my password saved? ▼
No, your password is only processed locally in your browser to check its strength; it is never stored or sent anywhere.
Q: Is it accurate? ▼
Yes, the tool uses established security rules and complexity algorithms to provide an accurate strength assessment.
